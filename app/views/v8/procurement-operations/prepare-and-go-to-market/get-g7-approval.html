Get G7 approval

Combine all the call off documents into a single pack and get approval from a G7.

Help with G7 approval

The G7 who will give approval will usually be your line manager. They will check:

  • All documents are included in the pack
  • The name of the school is correct in all places
  • The calculations in the spreadsheets are correct
  • The evaluation methodology is compliant with existing legislation
