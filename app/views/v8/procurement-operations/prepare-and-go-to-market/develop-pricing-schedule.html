Find the most up to date pricing schedule template from SharePoint (opens in new tab). Review the template to make sure it is suitable for the procurement.

If buying for multiple schools, each school should have its own tab on the pricing schedule Excel document.

Help with creating the pricing schedule

The pricing schedule is an itemised list making up the total contract value.

Make sure you think about longer term costs associated with this procurement, such as:

  • on-going support
  • inflationary increases
  • national living wage changes (for services contracts)
  • call-out costs for technicians and engineers
  • spare parts and replacements
  • capital outlay for equipment
