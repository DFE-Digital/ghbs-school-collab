Publish call off to competition

Publish the procurement to the relevant framework portal. Most of these will go on the framework providers portal.

If you are unsure where to publish, check the route to market information.

Help with publishing call off to competition

When publishing the procurement on the framework portal, you should use the option to notify all suppliers.

Clarification questions from potential suppliers should be answered in consultation with the school and responded to on the portal so it is available to all suppliers.

When selecting the duration of the tender process, think about how much information suppliers will need to consider and what they will be asked to provide.

It is recommended all tenders last for at least 20 working days.
