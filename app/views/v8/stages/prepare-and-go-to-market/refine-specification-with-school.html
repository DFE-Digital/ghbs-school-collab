Refine specification with school

Find out if there is a suitable specification template on SharePoint (opens in new tab). There is not a template available for all procurements. Check for any legislation which could affect the procurement to ensure compliance.

Remove any unnecessary sections and complete the specification based on the information the school has already provided. The specification can then be refined following a meeting with the school.

The specification should cover requirements for all schools.

Help with refining the specification

You can view all the cases for a school by reviewing the case details page (opens in new tab).

During the refinement sessions with the school, make sure all the requirements are collected in details. You should not just think about what the school needs right now from the supplier, but future needs as well, such as on-going support.
