Create Procurement Risk Assessment

Download the template, complete the procurement risk assessment then save it to SharePoint. This document is a way to highlight risk, but risk should be considered throughout the procurement.

Help with creating a procurement risk assessment

When creating a risk assessment consider:

  • the risk level of the procurement
  • how the route to market will affect the risk
  • if categories of spend affect the risk

This will be discussed at the Commercial Assurance Board (CAB). The board meet every Wednesday.
