Publish call off to competition

Publish the tender on the appropriate procurement portal. Ensure the whole pack is shared and all suppliers on the framework are notified.

You will need to monitor emails for questions from suppliers and liase with the school to answer them on the portal.

Help publishing call off to competition

When publishing on a 3rd part tender website there should be an option to notify all suppliers.

Any questions or requests for site visits should trigger a notification. Liase with the school to answer questions and post answers on the portal for all suppliers to see.

If applicable, you may want to arrange some dates for school visits ahead of time.

When choosing the publication's duration consider how much information suppliers need to digest. Complex procurements may need more time. It is recommended that suppliers are given at least 20 days for service procurements.
