Refine specification with school

Select the correct specification template from SharePoint (opens in new tab), and check for any legislation which could affect the procurement to ensure compliance.

Remove any unnecessary sections and complete the specification based on the information the school has already already provided.The specification can then be refined following a meeting with the school.

A specification is required for each school involved in the procurement.

Help with refining the specification

If a the school has previously used this service then you can look back over previous specifications in SharePoint (opens in new tab) .

During the refinement call with the school, make sure all the requirements are collected in details. You should not just think about what the school needs right now from the supplier, but future needs as well, such as on-going support.
