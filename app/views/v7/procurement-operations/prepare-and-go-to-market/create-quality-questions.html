Create quality questions

Choose the correct SharePoint template (opens in new tab), and work with the school to create questions that identify the best suppliers in a compliant way.

Consider setting word limits and using threshold (mandatory) or scored questions.

Help creating quality questions

Help schools convert their needs into compliant quality questions that identify the best suppliers.

Threshold questions are mandatory, and must be met for the supplier to be considered. Scored questions provide a way to compare answers between suppliers, but no supplier will be excluded regardless of score awarded.

Threshold questions normally start with "you must" and scored questions usually start with "demonstrate how you will".
