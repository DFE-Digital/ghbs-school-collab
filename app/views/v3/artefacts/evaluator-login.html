Complete procurement evaluation

Use this service to complete the supplier evaluation stage of the procurement. You will be able to:

  • Complete the declaration of interest form
  • Watch the evaluation training video
  • Complete the evaluation tasks

Bids must be evaluated using the pre-populated evaluation sheet with scores allocated using the scoring methodology and reasoning shared. Bids must be evaluated independently of each other and other evaluators.

Insufficient justification for scores will result in evaluations being returned, which could delay the procurement.
